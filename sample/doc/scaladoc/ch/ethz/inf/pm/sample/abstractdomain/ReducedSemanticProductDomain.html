ReducedSemanticProductDomain

class ReducedSemanticProductDomain [T1 <: SemanticDomain[T1], T2 <: SemanticDomain[T2], T <: ReducedSemanticProductDomain[T1, T2, T]] extends SemanticCartesianProductDomain[T1, T2, T]

The representation of reduced a Cartesian product supporting the operations of the semantic domain. After each semantic operation the reduction is applied. Note that this implementation is not particularly performant.
